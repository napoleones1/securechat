## ✅ v0.1 Alpha (COMPLETED) - March 2026

**Status:** Released ✅  
**Focus:** Core messaging & calling features

### Completed Features
- ✅ Authentication & user management
- ✅ Direct chat (1-on-1)
- ✅ Group chat dengan admin controls
- ✅ 5 message types (text, image, video, voice, file)
- ✅ Message status (sent, delivered, read)
- ✅ Voice & video call dengan WebRTC
- ✅ Call history & status tracking
- ✅ Admin panel untuk moderation
- ✅ Profile management
- ✅ Desktop app (Electron)
- ✅ Real-time features (typing, online status)
- ✅ File sharing (up to 50MB)

**Total Features:** 120+
